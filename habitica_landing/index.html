Gamify Your Life

Habitica is a free habit-building and productivity app that treats your real life like a game

Bring order to life

  • Track and manage your habits
  • Complete daily tasks
  • Set long-term goals and complete them

Addictive gameplay

  • Level up your character and earn rewards by completing daily tasks and achieving your goals in real life
  • Collect gold and armor to make your character more powerful
  • Build your own unique collection of pets and mounts

Reach your goals together

  • Create guilds, unite with friends, share achievements and achieve common goals together
  • Find new friends in a huge community, chat and collaborate to achieve mutual victories
  • Participate in annual seasonal events and get unique rewards

FAQ

First, you set yourself tasks that you need to complete in your daily life. As you complete these tasks in real life, you tick them off and earn Experience and Gold. Gold is used to buy equipment and other items, as well as rewards you create. Through experience, your character levels up and unlocks new content such as pets, skills, and quests! Details can be found in our step-by-step guide on the page Help -> Information for beginners.
